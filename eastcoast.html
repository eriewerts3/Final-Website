What you see in the East Coast

The Northeastern coast is famous for its various mountains and extensive forests. Cities like Boston, Philadelphia, and Manhattan all have a rich history behind them. These cities date back to the origins of our country and a variety of war sites can be visited. Travel to New York City and journey up or admire the architecture of the Empire State building. Take a hike with family or friends up the White Mountains, some of the largest mountains in the country.

The Southeastern coast is a rustic environment with beaches that leave you captivated.
